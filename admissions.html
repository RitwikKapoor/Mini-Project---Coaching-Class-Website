ADMISSIONS

How to Apply?

The courses at Genius Classes have a predefined specific percentage admission criterion. Broadly, for any course a student should fulfill percentage requirement for either Science & Math aggregate in class X Board / SSC examinations or PCB/PCM aggregate in class XII Board/HSC examinations.

So, a student, who had scored more than this percentage requirement, will be given direct admission into our course. However, if a student has not scored the required marks, then he/she will have to undergo an "Admission Test". Those students who qualify this test are then given admission to the applied course.

SCHOLARSHIPS

  • Expert Tuition Centre provides concession in fee for poor but intelligent and deserving students.
  • If a deserving students excels continuously three times in exams then he/she will be provided with scholarship benefits.
  • If a student scores 100% marks in (PCM/ PCB) , 50% of the fee will be remitted.
  • If a student scores 100% marks in any of the subject 50% of the fee will be remitted from that subject provided he studies that subject at the Expert Tuition Centre.
  • If a student scores above 95% marks in any of the subject 10% of the fee will be remitted from that subject provided he studies that subject at the Expert Tuition Centre.
  • Trophies are given every year to successful students who have come up with the flying colors.
